# Project Constitution: Smart Emergency Resource Locator

## 🌟 Mission
To empower the citizens of Hyderabad with instantaneous access to life-critical emergency resources, minimizing response times and saving lives through data-driven accessibility.

## ⚖️ Core Principles

1.  **Life First**: Every feature and data point must prioritize the speed and accuracy of locating emergency services.
2.  **Data Integrity**: We ensure that the resource information (Hospitals, Blood Banks, etc.) is accurate and verified.
3.  **Inclusivity**: The platform must be accessible and easy to use for all citizens, regardless of their technical proficiency.
4.  **Openness**: As a CivicTech project, we maintain transparency in our data sources and development processes.
5.  **Reliability**: The application must remain functional and responsive during high-stress, critical situations.

## 🛠️ Operational Rules

-   **Spec-Driven Development**: All new features must be defined in a specification before implementation.
-   **Compliance**: Maintain 100% compliance with Swecha and GitLab quality standards.
-   **Security**: Protect all user interactions and maintain a secure posture against common vulnerabilities.
-   **Testing**: No code shall be merged without comprehensive test coverage (Target: 100%).

## 👥 Governance
Developed and maintained by **Team MTSKV**. Decisions regarding the project's direction are made through consensus among the core team members, prioritizing the mission of public safety.
